## Getting Here: #### by public transit (recommended!): * Option 1: Take the A, B, C, or D subway line to 125 St. station. Walk about 10 min up St. Nicholas Terrace to the ASRC. * Option 2: Take the B or C subway line to 135 St. station. Walk about 6-8 min up through St. Nicholas Park to the ASRC. * Option 3: Take the 1 subway line to 137 St. station. Walk about 12-15 min up 135 St to the ASRC. * Option 4: Take the Metro North railroad to 125 St. station. From there, take the M100 or M101 bus or walk about 25 min from the station across Harlem to the ASRC. #### by car (not as recommended): * The ASRC does not have dedicated parking, but there is free street parking along St. Nicholas Terrace, adjacent to the ASRC. ## Once you reach the ASRC building: * Enter the ASRC on the **opposite side** of the building from St. Nicholas Terrace, in between the ASRC and the nearby Center for Discovery and Innovation (CDI). * Get signed in by security. If needed, call Dr. Keedy's office phone number (above), or call Hyacinth Camillieri at (212) 413-3244 or Jennifer Chow at (212) 413-3221. * Proceed up the elevator or stairs to the 3rd floor.
